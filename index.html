about GIN-global

When quality and reliability are required, GIN‑Global will always be your first choice!

GIN-Global is an international company with headquarter in Denmark and sister companies based in Houston, Texas USA and Atyrau, Kazakhstan.

We work with a number of multinational companies and organizations in the oil, mining and construction industries in many countries, supplying a range of products through contractual agreements.

We supply a wide range of products, such as:

  • 4-wheel-drive vehicles (4x4)
  • Passenger vehicles
  • Construction vehicles
  • Construction equipment
  • Auto parts for all types of vehicle
  • Automotive consumables along with various other technical items

Mission and vision

“Our mission is to provide your organization with the best possible service level – for us, it’s not rocket science, just common sense.”

Quality can be many things depending on where you are in the world and what kinds of products you are looking for. GIN-Global always tries to give you the best quality, including the service support and backup you need.

GIN-Global will always provide products to the required specifications and be able to support you with training or service backup.

GIN-Global are supplying and trading according to Incoterms and high Scandinavian standards, and we are always trying to do our best so that we will be your favorite supplier next time you need a vehicle or vehicle parts.
