GEHL Telehandler – Model TH10-55

The Gehl TH10-55 telehandler is a powerful and versatile machine crafted to tackle demanding construction and material handling challenges. This standard base unit is tailored for operators in the United States and Canada.

Combining strength, reach, operator comfort, and safety features, the Gehl TH10-55 is an excellent choice for professionals in construction, agriculture, and other industries. Its reliable performance makes it suitable for a variety of tasks.

Gehl TH10-55 Capacities

  • Lifting Capacity: 10,000 lbs (4535.92 kg)
  • Maximum Lifting Height: 55 feet 7 inches (16.95 meters)
  • Maximum Outreach: 42 feet 3 inches (12.88 meters)

The TH10-55's lifting capacity provides both reach and strength, ideal for moving heavy loads to elevated positions on multi-story sites. Its extensive outreach capability allows it to handle materials in challenging-to-reach areas.

Gehl TH10-55 Features

  • Engine: Powered by a Deutz TCD 3.6 L4 engine, delivering 127 horsepower (95 kW) and a maximum torque of 500 Nm at 2300 RPM. This engine ensures power and efficiency while adhering to the latest emission standards. It features a 5-speed powershift transmission for smooth operation and travel speeds up to 32 km/h.

  • Stability and Ease of Use: The long wheelbase of 11 feet 5 inches (3.5 meters), an overall width of 8 feet 4 inches (2.54 meters), and pneumatic tires provide excellent ground clearance of 1 feet 7 inches (0.48 meters), enhancing stability and ease of operation.

  • Controls: Equipped with ergonomic joystick controls for precise and effortless operation.

  • Cabin Design: The spacious cabin features Gehl’s SuperView, offering a 30% increase in forward visibility for safe operation, even when handling loads at higher elevations.

  • Safety: The operator station meets the latest safety standards, including FOPS (Falling Object Protective Structure) compliance, ensuring a safe working environment in challenging conditions.

  • Hydraulic System: The hydraulic system includes an axial piston pump capable of delivering 151 liters per minute at 275.80 bar pressure.

  • Safety Features:: Includes frame leveling of ±10 degrees to stabilize loads on uneven terrain.

  • Maintenance: The engine is perpendicularly mounted, providing 300-degree access to all major components, reducing downtime and facilitating quicker servicing.
